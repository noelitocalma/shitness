What is iMemo?
iMemo is an android-based mobile learning tool application that is designed for the students of present generation that depends too much to the technology. It can ease the students process of studying which can help them improve their knowledge on their lessons. The letter i in iMemo symbolizes one's self. Memo is short for memorize, and according to thesaurus the word memorize means commit to memory, remember or learn by heart. Its process which is memorization is the process of committing something to memory. iMemo focuses on the goal to change the educational system, thus it can be used by teachers to lessen the use of paper in terms of making reviewers and quizzes. Its objective is to support active learning experiences inside and outside of the classroom  Learning modules can be developed and accessed 24/7 online to introduce and reinforce the learning of concepts and foundational knowledge through the integration of text, and interactive element like quizzes.
